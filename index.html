Bottle Etching Animation

Initial tests for an animation project for Aberdeenshire Council, to raise awareness of the recycling process and encourage Aberdeenshire residents to work together to preserve the environment. The idea is to create 1 minute videos that focus on Paper, Tin Cans, Glass, and Plastic Packaging. Using these materials as the canvas for each animation and following characters through their journey will create a theme throughout each video and could be used throughout a variety of promotion, via print and online.
